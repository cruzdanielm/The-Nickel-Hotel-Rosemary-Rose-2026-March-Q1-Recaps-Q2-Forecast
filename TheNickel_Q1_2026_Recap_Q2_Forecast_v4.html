What it means

The Nickel is operating with sentiment indicators that property leaders typically take 18–24 months to earn — 4.9 on TripAdvisor, 4.9 on Google, and an internal Survey NPS 1.15× the 70-point goal. Section 1 · Takeaways

Sentiment is a strength worth marketing on.

Strengths

Survey NPS 80.8 (1.15× the 70-point goal). TripAdvisor 4.9 / Google 4.9 — top-tier on every public surface. Cleanliness 4.96 / Service 4.74 / Rooms 4.73 / Value 5.0 — all top of comp set. Service and Value rank #1 of 6.

Watchout

Internal review rating slipped 4.80 → 4.48 QoQ as volume scaled. Eight 1-star reviews this quarter, only 25% with a response. Location score dropped to 4.53 (#5 of 6).

Opportunity

4.9 / 4.9 / 100% Recommended is a marketing-grade trust signal. Lean into Service, Value, and Cleanliness in direct booking creative. Higher review volume on TripAdvisor unlocks ranking upside.

Next step

Stand up a 24-hour 1-star response SLA in Q2 and lift Google review-response coverage from 38% to 60%+. Drive TripAdvisor review volume to compound the 4.9 score into rank gains. Pre-arrival comms to set Location expectations on King St positioning.

Forecast risks & watch-items

Three things to monitor in Q2

ADR conviction. Apr forecast at $594 — $83 above March actual. Demand softness in any one weekend window could compress the month. Monitor pickup pace weekly.

Section 2 · Takeaways

Q1 ended ahead. Q2 is where the year gets made.

Strengths

Q1 NOI +$69K vs plan / +19.7%. March cleared budget on rate for the first time. F&B prime-cost ratio improved 430 bps vs plan. Compensation, Apartment Expenses, and Maintenance all favorable.

Watchout

Q1 ADR $35 below plan — March recovered, but the rate cushion is thin. Cash Flow Before Debt -$46K vs plan due to $115K of Other Non-Op (verified $0 budget — intentional zero).

Opportunity

Q2 forecast NOI of $1.37M is 3.3× Q1 actual NOI. May forecast NI +$152.9K — first standalone profitable month. Sustaining the Q1 prime-cost discipline through the volume ramp protects the entire forecast.

Next step

Lock the F&B labor ladder for the Q2 ramp. Tee up a mid-Q2 rate review once April pickup is in. Track Other Non-Op run-rate through Q2 to baseline against the verified $0 budget.
